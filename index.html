Data, definitions and reproducibility

The atlas keeps observed detector fixes distinct from inferred positions used only for continuity.

Catalogue

LPS v5.3.1 fixed-core contains 620,292 hourly linked positions grouped into 2,918 system identities. Of these, 442,479 are observed detector fixes and 177,813 are gap-posterior positions.

Coverage

Scope

Positions and intensity diagnostics are ERA5-derived. The catalogue is not an official best track.

Fixed-core continuity

Identities pass the frozen fixed-core observed-position strength and support rule. Posterior rows are retained to preserve temporal continuity but never count as observations and carry no detector physics.

Support screen

Strong, mixed and low support combine observed-position occupancy, the longest missing run and maximum linked speed. This is a review aid rather than proof of meteorological validity.

Map convention

Observed portions of a selected trajectory are solid; gap-posterior portions are dashed.

Metric definitions

  • Pressure deficit: environmental ring pressure minus local minimum MSLP, hPa.
  • MSLP depth: percentile ranking based on minimum mean sea-level pressure; lower pressure ranks stronger.
  • Smoothed vorticity: the catalogue's centre diagnostic.
  • 24 h precipitation: trailing track-centred catalogue diagnostic, mm.
  • Percentiles: fixed across all 2,918 v5.3.1 system identities; filters do not rescale them.

IBTrACS names

Named systems use NOAA IBTrACS v04r01 North Indian and Western Pacific best tracks. Matches use observed LPS fixes only and require credible time overlap, track separation and ambiguity margin. Unmatched systems use atlas class, genesis year and within-class annual sequence, such as DD 1986 03.

Unavailable fields

RH850 is null throughout this release and is omitted. Coincident state rainfall has not yet been recomputed for v5.3.1, so v5.2 values are not mixed into this atlas.

Download and provenance

Exports include stable v5.3.1 track IDs, units, filter state and continuity diagnostics. Selected-track downloads label each row as observed or gap-posterior and leave unavailable physics blank.
